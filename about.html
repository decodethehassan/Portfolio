Embedded IoT Developer & Firmware Engineer

I am an embedded IoT and firmware engineer with hands-on experience across STM32, ESP, Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA Jetson, Teensy, nRF tools, and industrial controllers. I build firmware for independent modules, test and debug hardware, and integrate embedded systems into production-ready solutions.
